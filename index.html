Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SiDaStuff Chess ("we", "us") operates chess.sidastuff.com. This policy explains what we collect and why. We aim to collect the minimum needed to run the site and to keep your data under your control.

What we collect

  • Account data. When you create an account, we store your email address and a Firebase-assigned user ID. We never see or store your password — authentication is handled by Firebase, which stores only a one-way hash.
  • Games you provide. PGN text and game links you paste or import are processed to run your review and coach analysis. We keep what you choose to save to your account so you can return to it.
  • Puzzle & training results. Your puzzle rating, solve history, and coach-game results, so we can track progress and serve puzzles at your level.
  • Usage & technical data. Anonymous counts (e.g. games analyzed, puzzles solved) and standard request data (IP, browser type) used to keep the service running and secure.

Where it lives

Account and training data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication and Realtime Database). Your board and engine preferences are saved in your browser's local storage and, when you're signed in, synced to your profile.

Cookies & local storage

We don't use advertising or tracking cookies. We use Firebase's authentication cookie/local storage to keep you signed in, and local storage to remember your settings and in-progress games. You can clear all of this from your browser or from Settings → Clear data.

Third parties

  • Firebase — authentication and data storage.
  • lichess.org & chess.com — only when you ask us to import your public games by username.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it for advertising.

Contact messages

If you use our contact form, we store the email, reason, and message you provide so we can respond. If you're signed in, we also record your user ID. These are visible only to site administrators and are used solely to reply to your request.

Public profiles

If you set a username, a public profile is created at chess.sidastuff.com/profile/<username> showing your username, plan flair, puzzle rating, and puzzle stats. Your email, games, and usage are never shown publicly. You can hide your profile by contacting us.

Your choices

You can delete your account and its data at any time by contacting us. You can use most of the site — reviewing games, playing the coach, solving puzzles — without creating an account.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email the address listed on the home page. We'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.
